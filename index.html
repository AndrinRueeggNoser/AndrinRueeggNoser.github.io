About us

We are 3 students from the cantonal school in Lee. Our names are Jana (16), Damaris (15) and Rania (14). We like to wear clothes that really suit us and our style.

That's why we came up with this idea. What we want is that as many people as possible can find their own style and have the chance to wear the clothes they imagine and feel comfortable in.

We do this because we have often been in the situation where we were looking for clothes but couldn't find any that met our expectations.

Our project

That's why we started a project where everyone can design their own clothes so they can really wear their own style and have a great filled wardrobe at home.

That's why we created this homepage, with which where you can create and buy your personal clothes easy at home.

Young peoples in our age are our goal group, but we are also happy when other people buy their clothes from us -by Uniquecorn!
